A Little About Me

Hi, I'm Tanner! I am a student of computer science at Weber State University, though this is my second time pursuing a bachelor's degree. I first went to BYU-Idaho and got a degree in Business Management. After a few jobs in that field, I realized I wanted to change things up. After a while I realized coding was the answer! So CS it is, and I'm doing a minor in Design for Digital Media so I can hone my creative skills in addition to the great coding stuff!

Getting to Know Tanner

If you could sit next to anyone on a plane, who would you choose?

I'd want to sit by Weird Al Yankovic, an artist I grew up listening to and whom I still look up to. What a weird dude, what a solid guy. My humor owes a lot to his music and general comedic style, but now that I've learned a lot more about music, I would want to ask him more technical questions that he never gets in interviews.
